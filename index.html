About Us

We are an up-and-coming business and we practice a hybrid model where we take both, studio and outstation/ freelance bookings. We use the highest quality of imported products from well renowned brands some of which include Huda Beauty, Jeffree Star Cosmetics, Fenty Beauty, Rare Beauty, Patrick Ta beauty, Charlotte Tilbury, NARS and many more.

Our aim is to provide the finest of experiences to all our brides on their big day. We go above and beyond for all our customers to make them feel welcome, cherished and appreciated. We believe in giving our clients a skin like finish which feels like second skin and to make women across the world feel more confident, strong and brave about who they are. Our goal is to give every one of our clients that moment of WOW and awe when they look at themselves for the first time after their makeover.

Our Vision

Our pursuit is to become the Creme de la creme of the beauty and personal care industry with customer sevice embeded into the core of our undertaking, listening to every opinion, feedback and testimonial that comes our way and make ourselves more adaptable and improved with every coming day.
